# leviosaPaperSubmission
1. The process this program implements to publish a technical journal.
	1) Technical journal committee starts calling for papers.
	2) Authors submits paper to technical committee.
	3) Technical journal committee sends the papers to the authors' managers. The managers should submit review comments.
	4) Technical journal committee invites experts in these areas to review these papers. The experts should submit review comments.
	5) Technical journal committee sends the feedback from the managers and experts to the authors.
	6) The authors submit updates of the paper based on the feedback.
	7) The technical journal committee starting the editorial work.
	8) After the technical journal is published, send an email to experts and authors to inform them regarding the gift.
